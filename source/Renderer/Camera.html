void setAspectRatio(const float aspect_ratio)¶

Set the aspect ratio This aspect ratio is given by a = w / h * fy / fx where fx and fy are the typical OpenCV camera parameters. If we have a perfect camera, then fy / fx = 1 and therefore a = w / h, i.e., the aspect ratio of the image.
